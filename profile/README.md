# taqelah! 🇸🇬

### Test Automation & Quality Engineering, *Lah!*

A name that blends technical excellence with Singaporean charm. **"Lah"** is that friendly, expressive word you'll hear all over Singapore — and it captures the spirit of friendliness and inclusivity we bring to everything we do.

---

## 🌱 Our Story

What began in **2018** as the **Singapore Appium Meetup** has grown into **taqelah!** — a thriving community for testers and automation enthusiasts passionate about quality engineering. Over the years, we've become a movement dedicated to lifting up Singapore's QA ecosystem.

## 🚀 Our Impact

- **50+ meetups** hosted
- **50+ speakers** from across the globe
- Insights across **test automation, AI testing, and quality culture**

From grassroots sessions to corporate collaborations, we've built bridges across Singapore's tech landscape.

## 🎯 Our Goals

- **Elevate** Singapore's QA and test automation standards through collaboration and learning
- **Inspire** and nurture young engineers to explore careers in quality engineering
- **Build** a quality-first culture across tech teams and organizations
- **Welcome** testers of all backgrounds and experience levels

## 🔭 Looking Ahead

Our vision is simple — to make Singapore a global hub for **Test Automation and Quality Engineering excellence.** Join us in uplifting the QA craft, one meetup and one conversation at a time.

## 💜 About the Company

**TAQELAH** is a mission-driven company building practical solutions to make technology more accessible and useful for people and communities.

While we're legally registered as a private limited company, we operate on a **not-for-profit model** — any surplus we generate is reinvested into:

- Making our products and services better for everyone
- Powering community-driven initiatives that make a real difference
- Keeping our platform accessible and affordable
- Ensuring the long-term sustainability of what we build together
- Empowering Singaporeans to grow their tech skills and advance their careers
- Welcoming people returning from career breaks with practical support
- Creating a safe, inclusive space where differently-abled and neurodivergent individuals can learn, grow, and thrive

> We believe businesses can be built with purpose — putting impact first, not just profit.
